About Us

We are a family-owned bakery specializing in fermented goods and artisan bread. With years of experience and a passion for natural ingredients, we strive to bring wholesome, flavorful products to our community.

Our Location

Originally founded in Argentina and later expanded to the United States, we now operate in two locations. Our Argentina location is currently undergoing renovations to better serve our customers.
